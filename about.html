About us

West Africa People's Institute (WAPI), Calabar, is a co-educational secondary school founded by Professor Eyo Ita — a pioneering Nigerian scholar and nationalist. His dream was to create an institution where academic excellence meets character formation and community service.
Located in the heart of Calabar, Cross River State, WAPI provides inclusive, high-quality education that blends intellectual growth with moral discipline, creativity, and leadership.
Our students are prepared for both national and international examinations — including WAEC, NECO, and JAMB. Beyond academics, we cultivate well-rounded individuals through sports, arts, debates, and community engagement.
Today, WAPI stands as a beacon of innovation, equipped with modern learning technologies to guide students into a connected global world.

Professor Eyo Ita

Founder, West Africa People’s Institute

A visionary educator and nationalist (1903–1972), Professor Eyo Ita believed that education was the key to African liberation and progress. His legacy continues to shape generations of leaders at WAPI.
